Infineon’s 1EDN7550/1EDN8550 EiceDRIVER™, 1-channel
low-side gate driver ICs, have truly differential inputs. Their control signal inputs are largely independent from the ground potential. Only the voltage difference between its input contacts is relevant. This prevents false triggering of power MOSFETs. Target applications are: server, telecom, DC-DC converters, telecom bricks, power tools, industrial SMPS, wireless charging, solar micro inverter
